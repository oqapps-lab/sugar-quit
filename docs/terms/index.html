1. What Sugar Quit is

Sugar Quit ("we", "the app") is a self-help app that supports people who want to reduce or quit added sugar. It includes a 90-day curriculum, daily check-ins, a craving-log, an AI coach for moments of urgency ("SOS"), and a streak tracker. It is NOT a medical device, NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, and NOT a treatment for diabetes, eating disorders, or any other clinical condition.
