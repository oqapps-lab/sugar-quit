# Sugar Quit — Journey Path (90-day Progress) screen

**Экран:** Прогресс 90-дневной программы. Где ты сейчас, что прошёл, что впереди.
**Почему этот:** не пересекается ни с The Exhale (forecast), ни с Zen Editorial (session). Уникальный Sugar Quit контент — рефлексия пути.
**Проверка:** формула применена к новому концепту с новой метафорикой, не переписан ни один успешный промпт.

---

## Промпт

```
Sugar Quit — AI craving coach. A 90-day neuroscience-backed program that guides you from active craving through mastery. Trigger prediction, daily practices, community support. For people who have decided to change and want a path to walk, not a promise to believe.

Mood & visual identity: imagine looking back over a long quiet walk at dusk — behind you the path is marked by stones and low plants you placed yourself along the way; ahead, the path continues into a soft misted distance you haven't reached yet. This app lives in that moment of looking back with quiet pride and forward with gentle curiosity. The palette is late-afternoon walking light — warm honey high at the horizon cooling through soft rose into deep plum-shadow at the edges, the colors of a path seen at magic hour. The gradient does the hierarchy: the path behind you glows warmer, the path ahead fades into cooler mist. Color temperature instead of progress bars.

A single long winding path IS the interface — you are always standing somewhere on it, past behind you in warmth, future ahead in soft mist. Milestones along the way are small natural markers — a stone, a low plant, a wooden post. Not badges. Memory anchors.

Today-you is the anchor — a small warm-saturated marker on the path at your current position, slightly brighter than everything else around it, the only element with full saturation. Beside it in minimal sans: "Day 47. You are here."

Typography is thoughtful editorial — thin serif for milestone names and chapter titles, clean sans for day counts and numbers, whisper-weight for dates and descriptions. Numbers are the heroes — days walked, milestones passed, days ahead. Large, at the surface, at rest.

One screen. The path at dusk.

At the top, a small soft exhale-glyph beside "Sugar Quit" in clean sans on the left, a small profile avatar on the right.

Below, tiny UPPERCASE TRACKED label in warm honey tone: "YOUR PATH."

Hero: editorial title — "You are on" in large sans on one line, then "Day 47 of 90" in massive italic serif on the next. A soft subtitle in whisper: "The path behind you is marked. The path ahead is written in soft light."

Below the hero, the path itself: a long gently curving vertical brass-colored thread running down through the center-right of the screen from top to bottom, thinner than you'd expect, warmer toward the upper portion and cooling into misted lavender-grey as it descends below today's marker.

Along the path, milestones at intervals — each a small natural marker with a label in thin serif beside it. In the warm upper section, behind today: a small honey-colored stone labeled "Week 1: First Decision" with "Feb 27" in whisper. A low rose-tinted plant labeled "Week 3: Identity Shift" with "Mar 13." A small wooden post labeled "Week 5: Mapping Your Triggers" with "Mar 27." Each with a small filled warm dot connecting it to the path.

At today's position — the anchor: a slightly larger coral-saturated marker, the only fully-saturated element on the path, with "Week 7: Meeting Your Triggers" in bolder italic serif and "Day 47 · You are here" in soft sans beneath.

Below today, the path cools and fades into misted lavender-grey. Future milestones appear as hollow outlined markers, no fill, no dates, only quiet labels in whisper-serif: "Week 9: Social Landscapes." "Week 11: The Long View." "Week 13: Release."  Each with a small "coming" whisper. The final marker at the bottom of the path is slightly larger: "Day 90: Threshold" in outlined serif, the destination visible but soft.

Floating in the warm zone to the left of the path, a small frosted-glass aside like a quiet footnote: "You have walked further than 68% of travelers reach by this day." In whisper serif, no decoration.

Toward the bottom of the screen below the path, a thin quiet stats row in warm honey tone: "47 CRAVINGS MET · $94 SAVED · 12 BREATHING SESSIONS" — tiny UPPERCASE TRACKED labels with small thin-sans numbers.

At the very bottom, a minimal pill-shaped navigation bar with four whisper icons — home / path (active, filled warm honey) / coach / profile.

Primary Design Surface: App.
```

## Что здесь СВЕЖЕЕ (не копия твоих промптов)

| Что из формулы | Заполнено СВЕЖИМ контентом |
|---|---|
| **Момент нужды** | "people who have decided to change and want a path, not a promise" — не "the moment you reach for sugar" и не "the split-second pause" |
| **Bodily moment (mood)** | "looking back over a long quiet walk at dusk" — не "slow exhale" и не "four-second gap" |
| **Nature palette** | "late-afternoon walking light, warm honey → soft rose → deep plum" — не "sky colors during golden hour" и не "seashell at rest" |
| **X-instead-of-Y** | "color temperature instead of progress bars" — не "instead of borders" и не "instead of progress bar" |
| **Functional aesthetic** | "path behind glows warmer, path ahead fades cooler" — не "warmer tones mean higher risk" |
| **Anchor** | "today-you as coral marker on the path, 'You are here'" — не "SOS I am HERE" и не "breathing orb Match me" |
| **Scene composition** | "long winding path through center-right, milestones at intervals, misted ahead" — не "gradient landscape with mountain peak" и не "quiet room with hero orb" |
| **Elements as metaphor-objects** | "stone / low plant / wooden post" — не "mountain peak / horizon line" и не "river stone / tree rings" |
