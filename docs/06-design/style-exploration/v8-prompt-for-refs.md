```
Sugar Quit — AI craving coach. Real-time conversations during cravings, trigger prediction based on time/stress/location, food alternatives, 90-day program, savings tracking, community support. For people who need support in the exact moment they reach for sugar.

Mood: the slow exhale after you successfully resist a craving — quiet pride, relief, calm. This app lives in that exhale.

Use the aesthetic of the attached reference images — the warm cream-and-amber gradient palettes, the frosted-glass cards, the big bold hero numbers, the multi-hue gradient orb accents, the clean rounded modern iOS polish, the minimal bottom tab bar. Build Sugar Quit's home screen in that visual world. Blend the references into one coherent warm wellness aesthetic.

One screen. Home.

At the top: a small greeting "Hello, Sarah" with "Today's rhythm" beneath in lighter text, a small avatar top-right, a pill showing "47d · 780pts" and a small bell icon with a dot.

The hero section: a large warm amber-to-cream gradient panel with "47 DAYS CLEAN" as a massive bold number — the single heaviest element on the screen — with a soft subtitle beneath ("your longest streak yet") and a small multi-hue gradient orb (coral, lavender, sage) floating to the right of the number like the psychology card in the reference.

Below the hero: a horizontal strip of three tiny frosted-glass mini-cards — THIS WEEK / 12 held — SAVINGS / $94 — MOOD / tender with a tiny cloud glyph. Minimal type, tight rhythm.

Below that: a pill tab row — Patterns / Triggers / Community — with "Patterns" active in a warmer filled pill.

Two frosted-glass cards side by side:
— Left: "Craving forecast" with "3:12 pm next window" and a small horizontal risk meter with a dot marked "now."
— Right: "This week" with "12 of 14 resisted" and a tiny seven-dot strip showing the days at a glance.

Below that: a wide frosted-glass card with a multi-hue gradient orb on the right side — "Today's practice / sitting with the discomfort" with a dark rounded pill button "Start today's session →" on the left.

Below that: a wide card "Your recovery year" with a small gently-rising trend curve and the line "Day 47 of 365 · 87% cleaner than month 1" with a forward arrow.

Below that: a horizontal layered bar chart titled "Why you crave" — STRESS 42% (coral), MEETINGS 21% (amber), SOCIAL 18% (peach), TIRED 12% (blush), BOREDOM 7% (cream). Thin bars, UPPERCASE mini-labels.

Below that: a row titled "Others walking with you" — three small circular avatars of fellow travelers with their streak numbers beneath (42d / 89d / 15d) and a "+more" indicator.

Above the bottom tab bar, a saturated coral wide pill button with a small flame glyph and the text "I am here — reach." The only fully-saturated element on the screen.

Bottom tab bar: Home (active, filled rounded icon) / Patterns / Coach / Profile — minimal, rounded, whisper labels.

Typography: follow the references — big bold hero numbers, small clean sans-serif, UPPERCASE only for tiny section labels.

Palette: warm cream, peach, amber gradients, coral for the SOS and accents, multi-hue gradient orbs as small decorative anchors. Warm dominant. No clashing green-on-red.

Primary Design Surface: App.
```
