# Scoring Summary: All 6 Research Projects

**Date:** April 7, 2026

---

## Final Rankings

### Tier 1: STRONG GO (empty market, clear demand)

#### 1. ShiftSleep
- **Category:** Health
- **Score:** 9.5/10
- **Market:** 22M+ shift workers in US, $435M-$1.9B TAM by 2034
- **Competition:** Practically empty. RISE doesn't support shift work. Timeshifter = jetlag only.
- **Unique Angle:** Only app built SOLELY for rotating schedule sleep planning (not tracking)
- **Monetization:** $5.99/mo or $49/yr + B2B (hospitals, fire departments)
- **SOM Y3:** $5-10M ARR
- **Risk:** Window closing — new entrants appearing since late 2025
- **Verdict:** Start immediately. First-mover advantage is real.

#### 2. SciatiCare
- **Category:** Health & Fitness
- **Score:** 9/10
- **Market:** 13M+ sciatica sufferers annually in US, $4.4-5.1B digital MSK market (CAGR 18%)
- **Competition:** B2C is empty. Kaia & Hinge Health are B2B only. Existing apps are garbage.
- **Unique Angle:** Sciatica-specific + office workers + phase-appropriate (acute/recovery/maintenance)
- **Monetization:** $7.99/mo or $49.99/yr
- **SOM Y3:** $3.75-7.5M ARR
- **Risk:** Regulatory (manageable under FDA 2026 wellness guidance)
- **Search gap:** 40-60K/mo search "sciatica exercises" but only 1-3K "sciatica app" = acquisition opportunity
- **Verdict:** Strong niche with clear expansion path to broader MSK.

---

### Tier 2: GO (strong opportunity, some execution risk)

#### 3. FamilyFlow (merged PairBudget)
- **Category:** Finance / Productivity
- **Score:** 8.5/10
- **Market:** 68M coupled households in US, $1.2-1.6B digital budgeting market
- **Competition:** Competitors are DYING. Honeydue dying, Zeta dead (May 2025), Mint dead (March 2024). No cross-platform splitting + budgeting app exists.
- **Unique Angle:** Splitting + budgeting + AI for couples. One subscription for two. No bank linking required.
- **Monetization:** $6.99/mo ($59.99/yr) for two
- **SOM Y5:** $15-30M ARR
- **Risk:** Speed to market — Monarch/YNAB could add splitting
- **Verdict:** Rare timing window. PairBudget and FamilyFlow should be MERGED into one product.

#### 4. DeskStretch
- **Category:** Health & Fitness
- **Score:** 8/10
- **Market:** 1B+ desk workers globally, $58B workplace wellness market (CAGR 6.7%)
- **Competition:** Wakeout (App of Year 2020) is iOS-only. Android wide open. No app combines cross-platform + video + body-part targeting + eye exercises + smart reminders.
- **Unique Angle:** Cross-platform, 2-5 min targeted video, desk-specific
- **Monetization:** $4.99/mo ($29.99/yr), B2B tier $5-10/user/mo
- **SOM Y3:** $2-8M ARR
- **Risk:** Retention / habit formation is the core challenge
- **Verdict:** Strong product opportunity, especially on Android. B2B is the growth lever.

#### 5. CarePaws
- **Category:** Pets / Health
- **Score:** 7.5/10
- **Market:** $2.8-3.2B pet care apps, 95M US households with pets
- **Competition:** General pet apps crowded BUT chronic illness niche is empty. Only 3 tiny apps exist.
- **Unique Angle:** Multi-species + multi-condition + vet integration + family coordination
- **Monetization:** $5.99/mo or $39.99/yr, B2B2C through vet clinics
- **SOM Y3:** $3.75-8M ARR
- **Risk:** Distribution — needs vet clinic partnerships for GTM
- **Verdict:** GO but GTM through vets is critical. Without it, discovery is the bottleneck.

---

### Tier 3: CAUTIOUS GO (high risk, saturated market)

#### 6. Habit Tracker (Social Accountability)
- **Category:** Productivity
- **Score:** 6.5/10
- **Market:** $1.7-1.9B (CAGR 13.5-15.2%)
- **Competition:** EXTREMELY crowded (hundreds of apps). Top players (Productive 18%, Streaks 14%) have no social features, but could add them.
- **Unique Angle:** Social accountability groups — scientifically proven +65% completion rate
- **Monetization:** $6.99/mo (above market average of $3.99-4.99)
- **Risk:** Cold start problem (social app needs groups from day 1), market leaders could add social features
- **Verdict:** Only proceed if you can solve cold start. Launch narrow (one niche), make social optional.

## Recommended Priority Order for Development

1. **ShiftSleep** — first-mover window, clearest gap
2. **SciatiCare** — similar positioning, can share health-app architecture
3. **FamilyFlow** — timing window (competitors dying), different category diversifies portfolio
4. **DeskStretch** — solid but less urgent (Wakeout not expanding to Android soon)
5. **CarePaws** — needs vet partnerships before dev, more preparation time
6. **Habit Tracker** — lowest priority, highest risk, most saturated

---

## Key Insight: ShiftSleep + SciatiCare Share DNA

Both are health apps for specific underserved niches with:
- Empty B2C competition
- Proven search demand
- Clear subscription model
- Phase-based content delivery
- Potential B2B upside

Consider building on shared architecture.
