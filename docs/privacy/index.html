Sugar Quit

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 May 2026

Sugar Quit is a personal coaching app. We collect the minimum data needed to give you a working app and a working subscription. We don't sell your data. We don't show ads. Most of your data lives on your device.

What we collect

1. Information you give us

  • Account info — email and password (hashed) when you sign in.
  • Onboarding answers — your goal, peak craving window, triggers, work environment, prior attempts. These shape your in-app coaching plan and stay tied to your account.
  • Usage data — daily check-ins, craving logs, SOS chat conversations, streaks. Stored locally; synced to your account if you're signed in.
  • Support messages — anything you write in the in-app Support form, or email us at support@sugarquit.app.

2. Information collected automatically

  • Device info — iOS / Android version, device model. Used for crash reporting and bug-fixing.
  • App-store identifiers — your subscription state via Adapty (described below).

3. What we do NOT collect

  • Your contacts, photos, location, microphone, camera, health data, calendar, or files.
  • Tracking IDs across other apps or websites.
  • Advertising identifiers — there are no ads in Sugar Quit.

Service providers we use

To run the app we share specific data with these providers under their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — stores your account email, password (hashed), and synced app state. Supabase privacy.
  • Adapty — manages your subscription state (free / trial / premium). Receives only your anonymous app-install ID and your purchase events. Adapty privacy.
  • Anthropic — powers the SOS chat coach. Receives your message text only when you tap Send in the SOS chat. Anthropic does not train on inputs from API customers. Anthropic privacy.
  • Apple App Store — handles billing for in-app purchases. Apple's standard privacy applies.

How we use the data

  • To run your coaching plan (using onboarding answers).
  • To remember your progress between sessions.
  • To respond to your SOS messages and Support questions.
  • To verify your subscription state.
  • To debug crashes and improve the app.

We do not use your data to train AI models, build advertising profiles, or sell to third parties.

Your rights

  • Access & export — Settings → Export my data lets you download a copy of everything tied to your account.
  • Delete — Settings → Delete account permanently removes your account and synced data.
  • EU / UK / California residents — you have additional rights under GDPR / UK GDPR / CCPA. Email support@sugarquit.app and we'll handle it within 30 days.

Children

Sugar Quit is intended for users 17 and older. We don't knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has given us data, email us and we'll delete it.

Security

Your password is salted and hashed (never stored as plain text). All app traffic uses TLS. We can't, however, guarantee absolute security — no service can.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy meaningfully, we'll notify you in-app before the change takes effect. The current version always lives at https://oqapps-lab.github.io/sugar-quit/privacy/.

Contact

Questions or requests: support@sugarquit.app.
